## Troubleshooting Download Issues

**"URL not recognized" error:**
- Ensure the URL starts with `https://www.instagram.com`
- Copy the URL directly from Instagram
- Try again with a fresh URL copy

**Download appears stuck:**
- Check internet connection
- Try a lower quality (360p vs 720p)
- Clear browser cache and retry

**Video won't play after download:**
- Try a different media player (VLC Media Player is universal)
- The file might be corrupted—try downloading again
- Ensure your device supports MP4 format

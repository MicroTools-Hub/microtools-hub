**On Mobile:**

## Common Download Issues and Solutions

**"URL not recognized" error:**
- Ensure the URL starts with `https://www.tiktok.com` or `https://vt.tiktok.com`
- Copy fresh from TikTok app or browser
- Try the full URL instead of shortened link

**Download appears to hang:**
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different quality setting
- Clear browser cache and retry
- The video might be loading—wait 30 seconds

**"Video not available" message:**
- The video may have been deleted
- Account might be private
- Geographic restrictions may apply
- Try the URL again; temporary issues are common

**Video won't play after download:**
- Use a universal media player (VLC Media Player)
- Ensure your device supports MP4 format
- Try downloading the video again

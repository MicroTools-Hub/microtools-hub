## Common X Download Errors and Solutions

**"URL not recognized" error:**
- Ensure the URL starts with `https://twitter.com` or `https://x.com`
- Copy the full URL from the browser address bar
- Verify it's a video post (not a link to external video)
- Try again with a freshly copied URL

**Download appears stuck or slow:**
- Check internet connection
- Try a lower quality setting (360p vs 720p)
- Clear browser cache
- Some videos take 30–60 seconds; wait longer

**"Video not available" message:**
- The video may have been deleted
- Tweet was possibly removed
- Account might have been suspended
- Your region might have access restrictions

**Downloaded video won't play:**
- Try a universal media player (VLC Media Player)
- Ensure your device supports MP4 format
- The file might be corrupted—download again
- Update your media player

## Troubleshooting Download Issues

**"URL not recognized" error:**
- Ensure the URL starts with `https://www.youtube.com/shorts/`
- Copy the URL directly from the browser bar, not the share button
- Refresh and try again

**Download appears stuck:**
- Check internet connection
- Try a lower quality (360p instead of 480p)
- Clear browser cache and retry

**Video missing after download:**
- Check your Downloads folder (default location)
- Ensure your device has enough storage space
- Try downloading again with a different quality
